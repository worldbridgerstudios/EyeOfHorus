## Why These Three?

### x (Cosmogenic) — 17 occurrences

- Uvular fricative — deepest throat position
- Greek could NOT absorb this sound
- Preserved in decan names as archaic
- **Meaning:** Universal principle, cosmic scale

### d (Phylogenic) — 10 occurrences  

- Voiced alveolar stop
- Merged with t in Late Egyptian (pre-Greek contact)
- Verb: **DO** — the action phoneme
- **Meaning:** Species pattern, collective action

### k (Ontogenic) — 5 occurrences

- Velar stop
- Merged with g in Late Egyptian
- Verb: **CYCLE** — individual return
- **Meaning:** Personal cycle, individual scope
